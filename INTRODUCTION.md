# AIScope SG Introduction | 项目介绍

## English

AIScope SG is a Singapore-focused AI Job Exposure Index.
It estimates how strongly each occupation may be affected by AI in the near-to-medium term, with local context built in.

Unlike generic global benchmarks, AIScope SG emphasizes Singapore-specific realities:
- regulated professions
- Progressive Wage Model (PWM) coverage
- work-from-home feasibility
- practical constraints for on-site and physical jobs

The project is designed to be understandable to both technical and non-technical audiences.
It combines data visualization with explainable scoring so users can quickly compare occupations and explore the reasons behind each score.

AIScope SG is developed as a public-interest initiative to support:
- career planning
- policy discussion
- media interpretation
- skills and reskilling conversations

## 中文

AIScope SG 是面向新加坡的 AI 职业暴露指数项目。
它用于评估不同职业在中短期内受到 AI 影响的程度，并融入新加坡本地语境进行解释。

与通用的全球模型不同，AIScope SG 强调新加坡特有维度：
- 职业监管与执照约束
- 渐进式薪资（PWM）保护
- 远程办公（WFH）可行性
- 现场与体力岗位的现实替代限制

项目目标是让技术与非技术用户都能读懂：
既能快速看到可视化结果，也能查看每个职业评分背后的原因。

AIScope SG 作为公益项目，主要服务于：
- 职业规划
- 政策讨论
- 媒体解读
- 技能提升与转型决策
