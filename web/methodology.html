AIScope SG Methodology

Transparent scoring framework for Singapore AI Job Exposure Index

1. Core Score

Each occupation receives an AI exposure score on a 0-10 scale. The baseline is estimated from task automability, language dependence, workflow digitization readiness, and substitution speed.

2. Singapore Local Protection Factors

  • PWM constraint: PWM-covered occupations apply a hard risk cap to reflect wage-floor protection and physical task isolation.
  • Regulatory moat: SAL, MOH, and MAS licensing barriers reduce short-term replacement exposure.
  • Work context: on-site physical presence and multi-lingual frontline communication can reduce pure automation risk.

3. Explainability and Audit Trail

Every occupation includes source metadata in source_meta: SSOC version, wage statistic year, and LLM model used for scoring.

4. Intended Use

AIScope SG is designed for policy research, workforce transition planning, and public education. It is not legal advice or a formal determination of employment rights.
